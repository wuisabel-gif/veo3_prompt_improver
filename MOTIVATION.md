# Why I Built Real

Real didn't start as a product. It started as a question I couldn't stop asking: what happens when someone with no background in filmmaking suddenly has the tools to build cinematic worlds?

For most of my life, two sides of me lived in separate rooms. I spent my days in mathematics, physics, and computer science, and I loved them. But I also loved music, beauty, fashion, and the worlds artists build around their work. I just wasn't part of those worlds. I wasn't a fashion editor. I wasn't a filmmaker. I wasn't a music-video director. I was a student, so those interests mostly stayed in my imagination.

Then 2025 happened, and AI video generation got good fast. When Veo 3 arrived, the wall between those two rooms suddenly had a door in it.

So I started experimenting on my [Bella PI YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@BellaPi314). One video became ten. Ten became fifty. Fifty became a hundred: fashion films, editorials, runway concepts, music visualizers. Somewhere past two hundred videos, I noticed something. The shots that actually worked weren't luck. The same patterns kept showing up behind them, again and again: how to frame a subject, which lens to name, how to set a palette, how to pace a clip, how to weave an aesthetic through every line.

Here is the thing nobody tells you about AI video. The hard part isn't generating something. It's directing the model toward the exact thing in your head. My early prompts missed because they were vague. The ones that landed were specific, structured, and written like a creative director's brief.

Real is that lesson, organized. It takes a rough idea and turns it into a structured, ultra-cinematic Veo 3 brief, using the same patterns I learned the slow way, packaged so anyone can reach them in seconds.

The numbers from the channel are nice (more than 200 videos, over 177,000 views, hundreds of subscribers), but they were never the point. The point is the door. It's the feeling that you can reach into a world you were never "part of" and make something that belongs there.

That's why Real exists: so more people get to feel it too.

Make it real.
